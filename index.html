ACCESS/S1S Summer School Enrollment

The ACCESS Program in Orange County provides resources for high school students to earn their diploma and provide college readiness for students who cannot otherwise attend traditional classes.

The electronic application for Summer School courses is done using a Docusign, where students parents and counselors can enroll in summer coursework and counselors retain their application and signatures electronically. The Software Applications team at OCDE developed the forms and templates and I created the bulk import templates to deliver the applications to over 14,000 students in eleven high school districts in Orange County.

The data is extracted using Docusign Retrieve and housed in a Data Warehouse. We provide set up for the extracting the data, providing real-time application status in Tableau reports and daashboards. The adminiistrators can then export the results of Complete/Incomplete status' of the application process.

SharePoint is a Microsoft Content Management System (CMS) used to build and organize websites and its content. I administer the creation of pages, content and users of the thousands of pages created by OCDE, and I add minor customizations to web pages, both in our Intranet and public facing site.

Although we have a framework where users primarily add objects that stack in block format by default, I will also add CSS into the elements to extend its design capability. I also provide remote learning and troubleshooting of any issues users may have, either via email or in the event of a critical issue, remote sessions where I can make corrections in real time.

I also manage several Wordpress websites for special groups who have hired third party vendors to develop a site with the Wordpress platform. I implement the security updates to the site and numerous plugins. It can be a challenge, especially with the number of different templates and plugins that I must learn to quickly understand and manage. On one occasion, a site was completely down and the group who used it could no longer log into the Admin location.
